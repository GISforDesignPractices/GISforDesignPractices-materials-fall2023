Add a Mapbox style to Felt

  1. Publish your style from the previous part.
  2. Find the style's URL:
    1. Go to Share
    2. Under Developer Resources select Third Party > Carto.
    3. Copy the Integration URL.
  3. Open a Felt map.
  4. Click on the basemap selector to change it, select From URL and enter the URL you created above in the Tile URL field.
