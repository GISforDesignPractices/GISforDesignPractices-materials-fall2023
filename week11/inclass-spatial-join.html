In-class Exercises

Spatial Joins

  1. Download today's data.
  2. Open New York City Bike Routes.geojson in QGIS.
  3. Open Council District Boundaries in QGIS.
  4. Intersect the two files to get a bike routes file where the bike routes are clipped to the district boundaries. Use Vector > Geoprocessing > Intersection and select the two files, bike routes as input and council distrcts as overlay.
  5. Change your project's length units by going to Project > Properties > General and changing Units for distance measurement to miles.
  6. Use the field calculator to add a length field to the file.
  7. Search in the bottom left for Join attributes by location (summary).
  8. In this window select:
    1. Base layer: Council District Boundaries
    2. Fields to summarise: length (as calculated above)
    3. Summaries to calculate: sum
  9. Make a choropleth with the result.
