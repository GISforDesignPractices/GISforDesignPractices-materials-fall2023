Assignment 8

More Felt

Create a new map in Felt. The map can be on any topic and use any data you choose, including data for your final project. Consider using the Felt QGIS plugin when uploading your laeyers. Then do the following:

  1. Add or edit at least one zoom-based styling rule. That is, your features' styles should change somehow according to the map's zoom level.
  2. Consider how the map will look when shared online. Give thought to the title, description, and pop-ups you use. Modify your pop-up's included attributes and labels as applicable to make them more readable.
     

Submitting your work

Please submit a link and explanation of your work, including your intent and any difficulties you might have run into.
