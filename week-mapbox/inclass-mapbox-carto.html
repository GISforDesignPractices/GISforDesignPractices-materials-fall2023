Add a Mapbox style to Carto

  1. Publish your style from the previous part.
  2. Find the Carto URL
    1. Start with this template: 
      https://api.mapbox.com/styles/v1
  /YOUR_USERNAME/YOUR_STYLE_ID
  /tiles/256/{z}/{x}/{y}
  ?access_token=YOUR_MAPBOX_ACCESS_TOKEN
    2. Replace YOUR_USERNAME with your Mapbox username.
    3. Replace YOUR_STYLE_ID with your Mapbox style id. When you're looking at your style in Mapbox studio the URL is like this: 
      https://studio.mapbox.com/styles/YOUR_USERNAME/YOUR_STYLE_ID/edit/#14.4/40.71989/-73.98799
    4. Replace YOUR_MAPBOX_ACCESS_TOKEN with your Mapbox access token. This is in the Share window in Mapbox Studio.
  3. Open a Carto map.
  4. Click on the basemap to change it, select Mapbox for the source, and enter the URL you created above.
