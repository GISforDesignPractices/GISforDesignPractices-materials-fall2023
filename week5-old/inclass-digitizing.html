In-class Exercises

Digitizing

  1. We are going to create a new shapefile that edits the Borough Boundaries shapefile to show the Brooklyn Navy Yards shoreline in 1924.
  2. Reopen you map document where you georeferenced the Brooklyn Navy Yard.
  3. On the Search QMS toolbar, search for orthophotography, find and add the NYC.gov tiles orthophotography 1924
  4. Add the Borough Boundaries shapefile
  5. You should now have a mapdoc with Google Hybrid base layer, 1924 orthophoto, Borough Boundaries, and your rectified Brooklyn Navy Yard map.
  6. Export a copy of the Borough Boundaries shapefile (nybb) to week6/data/processed/nybb_navy_yards_1924.shp
  7. Right-click your new layer to Toggle Editing
  8. Zoom in to the Brooklyn Navy yards shoreline, make the the nybb_navy_yards_1924 shape semi-transparent so that you can see the 1924 ortho photo through the polygon.
  9. On the digitizing toolbar, select the Vertex Tool
  10. Wait here and I'll walk you through the next step.
  11. Click on the nybb_navy_yards_1924 shape and edi the shape to match the 1924 shoreline in the Navy Yards. You do this by clicking on each vertex and moving it.
  12. Once you have the desired shore outline, right-click on the shapefile in the Layers panel and deselt Toggle Editing, save your changes.
  13. Toggle back and forth between the current Borough Boundaries and the 1924 Borough Boundaries to see how the shoreline changed.
  14. You know have a new shapefile of the 1924 shoreline.
