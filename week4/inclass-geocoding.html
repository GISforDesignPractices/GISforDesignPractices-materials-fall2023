In-class Exercises

Geocoding

  1. In today's data folder, open the CSV file called "favorite tacos in NYC" in Excel or Google Sheets. What do you notice?
  2. Go to the Batch Geocoder for NYC and upload the CSV.
  3. In "Indicate Bound," stick with Citywide. For "Select a column," make sure to pick street address, and click the button "Add to geocoder."
  4. Feel free to check the results, and then download the CSV. Open it again in Excel or Google Sheets. What do you notice? What has changed?
  5. Open QGIS and add the Borough Boundaries shapefile.
  6. Now add your geocoded "favorite tacos in NYC" as a Delimited text layer.
